01
E-commerce Revenue Scale
DTC Fashion Brand · Bangladesh
+60% Revenue
🔴 Problem
A DTC fashion brand was spending BDT 40k/month on Facebook Ads but only earning BDT 32k back — a 0.8× ROAS. Their website had 4,000 monthly visitors but a 0.7% conversion rate. No email list, no retargeting, no follow-up system.
🔵 Strategy
Rebuilt the entire Meta Ads account: killed 11 underperforming ad sets, created 3 new audience segments (lookalike, interest, retargeting). Wrote and tested 6 ad creatives in 2 weeks. Launched a 5-step abandoned cart email sequence via Mailchimp. Set up Google Shopping for high-intent searches.
🟢 Result
ROAS climbed from 0.8× → 1.6× (week 2) → 2.4× (week 4) → 3× (week 6). Monthly revenue increased 60% in 90 days without increasing the ad budget. The abandoned cart sequence alone recovered 28% of lost sales. Client renewed for a 6-month retainer.

SEO Traffic Explosion
SaaS Startup · South Asia
+220% Traffic
🔴 Problem
A B2B SaaS startup was paying $3,200/month in Google Ads just to get 80 leads. They had zero organic presence — ranking page 4–6 for their 3 most important keywords. Content output was 1 blog post per quarter with no keyword strategy.
🔵 Strategy
Ran a technical SEO audit using Ahrefs — found and fixed 47 critical issues (broken links, missing meta tags, slow page speed). Built a topic cluster map targeting 120 keywords across 8 content pillars. Produced 3 long-form articles/month (1,800–2,500 words each) optimised for featured snippets.
🟢 Result
Month 1: +18% traffic. Month 3: +90%. Month 5: +220%. 18 keywords now on page 1, including their 2 highest-value terms. Monthly organic leads went from 0 to 47. The client cut their Google Ads spend by 40% — saving $1,280/month — while total leads increased.

03
Paid Ads CPA Reduction
Fintech Client · Europe
−35% CPA
🔴 Problem
A European fintech company was managing €50k/month across Google and Meta. Their CPA was €48 — too high to scale profitably (target was €30). They had never run a proper A/B test. Same creatives had been running for 6+ months with no refresh.
🔵 Strategy
Set up a structured A/B testing framework: tested 4 headlines, 3 landing page variants, and 6 audience segments simultaneously. Paused all stale creatives. Moved 60% of budget to exact-match, high-intent Google Search keywords. Rebuilt Meta campaigns using conversion-objective campaigns instead of traffic.
🟢 Result
CPA dropped from €48 → €38 (week 3) → €31 (week 6) → €31.2 avg (week 8) — hitting the target. Lead volume grew 22% at the same time. ROAS went from 1.9× to 3.2×. Client increased budget to €80k/month and has retained me for 14 months straight.
